Toolboxes and related structures¶

Content types and custom entities¶

The toolboxes platform relies on 4 content types: * portal_main_page * toolboxes * toolbox_tool * toolbox_tool_step

See also End user toolbox documentation

Relies on¶

Currently relies on:

  • Contributed modules:
    • token_custom allows us to create custom snippets within legal content
    • paragraphs
    • voting_api
    • fivestar
    • block_reference
  • Custom modules:
    • ilao_portal_pages
  • Taxonomies:
    • legal_issues
